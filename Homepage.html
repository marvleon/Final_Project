About Me

Hi! My name is Marvin Leon, I'm an undergraduate student at Portland State University majoring in Computer Science. I previously attended Concordia University. Following their closure I transferred to PSU. I'm currently a part of the UNST mentor program. I have the opportunity to create lesson plans and facilitate my own mentor sessions for Freshman Inquiry courses. In my role, I enjoy connecting, advocating, supporting, and learning with students. During the summer, I work at a non-profit summer camp and in my free time I like to use my sewing machine to repair and make garments. In the future, I hope to continue my role as an educator in Computer Science. Thanks for visiting my site!
